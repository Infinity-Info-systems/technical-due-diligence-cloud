# Executive Summary Template

## Summary

Provide a short, decision-ready summary of the platform’s due diligence position.

## Key Takeaways

- what is strong
- what is at risk
- what matters most for the decision
- what should happen next

## Leadership Ask

- approve
- remediate
- negotiate
- re-review

## Executive Rule

Keep this summary short enough for a leadership readout but specific enough that the next action is obvious.
# Executive Summary Template

## Purpose

Use this template to summarize the diligence conclusion for senior stakeholders.

## Suggested Sections

- platform overview
- material risks
- investment implications
- remediation priorities
- recommendation

## Use

Use this template when the audience needs the decision, not the full evidence trail.

## Outcome

The summary should make it easy to see whether the platform is acceptable, what needs fixing, and what should happen next.
