# SaaS Platform Risk Model

## Purpose

This model focuses on the risks that matter most when evaluating a SaaS platform.

## Risk Areas

- scalability
- tenancy and isolation
- operations maturity
- resilience
- security
- data portability

## How To Use

- identify whether the risk is design, process, or operating-model based
- assess whether the risk affects adoption or valuation
- prioritize the highest-exposure items first
