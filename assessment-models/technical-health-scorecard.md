# Technical Health Scorecard

## Purpose

Give reviewers a compact view of the platform health across the major diligence areas.

## Score Areas

- architecture
- reliability
- security
- DR
- FinOps
- operating model

## Use

Use this scorecard when you need a compact summary that can be discussed quickly with both technical and executive stakeholders.

## Example Scale

- 1 = weak
- 2 = emerging
- 3 = defined
- 4 = managed
- 5 = strong
