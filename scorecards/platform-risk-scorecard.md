# Platform Risk Scorecard

## Purpose

Summarize the top platform risks that should shape diligence findings and follow-up work.

## Risk Categories

- critical dependency risk
- control risk
- availability risk
- cost risk
- modernization risk
- operating model risk

## Use

Rank the risks by business materiality so the follow-up work reflects real decision pressure, not just technical severity.

## Use

- rank risks by materiality
- identify risks that affect valuation or transition confidence
