# Cloud Maturity Scorecard

## Purpose

Summarize cloud maturity in a way that is easy to discuss with investment and leadership stakeholders.

## Dimensions

- governance
- architecture
- reliability
- security
- cost control
- operating cadence

## Use

This scorecard should show whether the platform is mature enough to support the intended business growth or transition plan.

## Interpretation

- low score indicates elevated diligence risk
- mid score suggests a platform that needs standardization
- high score indicates a mature and supportable platform
