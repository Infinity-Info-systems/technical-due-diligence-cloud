# Architecture Risk Review

## Purpose

Review architecture risk to understand whether the platform can scale, change, and remain supportable.
This is one of the highest-value diligence areas because architecture issues often drive future cost, delay, and operational risk.

## Review Topics

- dependency complexity
- design fragility
- scalability limits
- technical debt
- integration risk
- vendor lock-in exposure
- platform extensibility

## Questions To Ask

- Where are the brittle dependencies?
- Which services are mission critical?
- What parts of the architecture are hard to change?
- What modernization work is blocked by design choices?

## Use

Use this page when you need to understand whether the current design will hold up under growth, change, or integration pressure.

## Outcome

Architecture risk should tell you whether the platform can evolve without creating expensive rework or operational fragility.

## Evidence To Collect

- architecture diagrams
- platform inventory
- dependency map
- technical debt notes
- change history
- integration catalog

## Interpretation

Architecture risk should be judged by how much it limits future change, not just by how current the design appears.
