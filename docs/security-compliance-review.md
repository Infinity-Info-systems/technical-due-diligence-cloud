# Security And Compliance Review

## Purpose

Assess the security posture and compliance readiness of the platform from a diligence perspective.

## Assessment Areas

- identity and access controls
- data protection
- logging and monitoring
- policy alignment
- exception management
- audit evidence readiness
- third-party exposure

## Questions To Ask

- Are the key controls implemented and tested?
- Are exceptions tracked and approved?
- Can the team show evidence quickly?
- Are regulated data flows understood?

## Evidence To Collect

- control records
- policy documents
- audit evidence
- exception logs

## Use

This review should make it obvious whether the platform has a control environment that can stand up to scrutiny.
