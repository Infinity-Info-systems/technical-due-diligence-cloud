# DR Readiness Assessment

## Purpose

Assess whether the platform can recover within agreed business time objectives.

## Assessment Areas

- recovery planning
- dependency mapping
- backup and restore testing
- failover readiness
- evidence retention

## Questions To Ask

- Are the critical services identified?
- Are RTO and RPO realistic?
- When was recovery last tested?
- What dependencies affect recovery?

## Evidence To Collect

- DR plans
- test results
- restore evidence
- failure scenario notes
