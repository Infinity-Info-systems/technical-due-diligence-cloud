# Reliability Assessment

## Purpose

Assess how well the platform is prepared to sustain service availability and recover from incidents.

## Assessment Areas

- service ownership
- SLO coverage
- incident management
- observability quality
- response learning loop

## Questions To Ask

- Which services matter most?
- Are SLOs defined and monitored?
- Are incidents reviewed consistently?
- What recurring patterns still exist?

## Evidence To Collect

- incident reports
- SLO documents
- monitoring dashboards
- post-incident actions
